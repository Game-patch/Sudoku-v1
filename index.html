How to Play Sudoku

Objective: Fill the entire grid so that each row, column, and 3x3 box contains the numbers 1 to 9 exactly once.

  • The puzzle is a 9x9 grid made of nine 3x3 boxes.
  • Some numbers are pre-filled and cannot be changed.
  • Fill empty cells with numbers 1–9.
  • No duplicates in:
    • Any row
    • Any column
    • Any 3x3 box
  • Use logic and patterns — guessing leads to mistakes!
  • Take your time. It’s about accuracy, not speed!
